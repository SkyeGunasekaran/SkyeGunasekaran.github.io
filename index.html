About

I am an undergraduate researcher at UC Santa Cruz advised by Prof. Jason Eshraghian in the Neuromorphic Computing Group. My research spans scalable LLM pretraining infrastructure, transformer efficiency optimizations, and biologically-inspired learning algorithms grounded in predictive coding.

My work has appeared in Nature Communications, Nature Computational Science, and APL Machine Learning. I also lead a 150+ student ML research curriculum at UCSC Baskin Engineering and run an undergraduate research incubator. I am a Goldwater Nominee and NSF REU fellow, and expect to graduate in June 2026.
