About

I am an undergraduate researcher at UC Santa Cruz advised by Prof. Jason Eshraghian in the Neuromorphic Computing Group. My research focuses on bridging the gap between the efficiency of biological intelligence and the power of large-scale foundation models to deliver pareto-optimal clinical outcomes.

My work has appeared in ICLR Nature Communications, Nature Computational Science, and APL Machine Learning. I have lead a 150+ student ML research curriculum at UCSC and run an undergraduate research incubator. I am a Goldwater Nominee and NSF REU fellow, and expect to graduate in June 2026.
